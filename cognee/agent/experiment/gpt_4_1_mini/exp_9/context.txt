#### Common Failure Modes:

- **Wind Speed Calibration:** Temporarily remove the propeller and connect to an Anemometer Drive to verify and adjust settings based on propeller RPM.

- **Periodic Checks:** Regularly check mechanical rotations and electrical outputs.
- **Potentiometer Replacement:** Replace potentiometer after long use (50 million revolutions) if signals are noisy or nonlinear.
- **Bearing Replacement:** Check and replace bearings if noise increases or wind speed thresholds change.
- **Grounding Maintenance:** Ensure proper grounding to avoid static discharge, which can damage the transducer.
